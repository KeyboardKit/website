A custom keyboard can replace the standard system keyboard on iOS & iPadOS. It's the only way for an app to directly interact with other apps, and expose its unique features.

KeyboardKit lets you create a custom keyboard in minutes, using technologies like Swift & SwiftUI. It's open-source, has many features and can be extended with KeyboardKit Pro.
