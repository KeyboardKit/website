Welcome to KeyboardKit!

KeyboardKit lets you create custom keyboards for iOS and iPadOS. It supports a ton of functionality and can be styled with custom colors and effects.

The app lets you create custom keyboards directly on your iPhone and iPad. If you are a developer, you can use the open-source library and Pro extension to create your own apps.
